Hospital Stay Patterns
	•	The average hospital stay was 15 days, indicating moderately long inpatient treatment durations across the dataset.
	•	The longest stays reached 30 days, suggesting potential outlier cases involving more complex or prolonged treatment.
	•	Admissions varied over time, with the highest number occurring in August 2020 and the lowest in May 2019, indicating potential seasonal or operational variation in hospital demand.
 
Gender and Medical Conditions
	•	The most common condition among female patients was arthritis, while diabetes was the most common condition among male patients.
	•	Overall, the top three conditions differed slightly by gender:
	◦	Female: Arthritis, Diabetes, Obesity
	◦	Male: Diabetes, Hypertension, Asthma
	•	Despite these differences, overall admission volumes were approximately equal between male and female patients.
 
Medication Patterns
	•	Analysis of prescriptions by condition showed no significant gender imbalance in medication prescribing.
	•	The proportion of female prescriptions across conditions was generally consistent, suggesting similar treatment patterns between genders.
 
Hospital-Level Analysis
	•	When analysing admissions by hospital, Smith hospital recorded the highest number of admissions, while Abbott recorded the lowest.
	•	At Smith hospital:
	◦	Arthritis had the highest average cost per day (~4654) despite having a similar average stay length to other conditions.
	◦	Average stay durations across major conditions were consistent (~15 days).
 
Admission Type Trends
	•	Different conditions appeared across urgent, emergency, and elective admissions, with some variation in frequency by condition.
	•	Obesity had the highest number of urgent admissions, while diabetes appeared most frequently in emergency admissions.
	•	Arthritis appeared most frequently in elective admissions.
 
Test Result Outcomes
	•	Test results were fairly evenly distributed across admission types, with most combinations representing roughly 11% of total admissions.
	•	Elective admissions produced the highest number of abnormal and normal results, while emergency admissions produced the lowest counts overall.
 
Treatment Cost Patterns
	•	High-cost treatments were least common across all admission types, suggesting that most hospital stays fall into low or medium cost categories.
	•	The average billing amount was ~25,539, while the average daily treatment cost was ~3,386, implying that most hospital stays involve multiple days of treatment rather than single-day procedures.
